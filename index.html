Privacy Policy – Texting Story : Chat Master

Last updated: November 29, 2025

Thank you for playing Texting Story : Chat Master! We take your privacy very seriously. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why, and how you can control it — written in plain, honest language.

1. We Do NOT Collect Personal Information

All your stories, messages, choices, and progress are stored only on your device. We never see your chats, your name, email, Apple ID, or any personal details.

When you delete the app → everything disappears forever.

2. Data Collected for App Improvement & Ads

We use two trusted Google services:

Firebase Analytics (Google)

Helps us understand how the app is used so we can make it better. This includes:

  • Which stories are most popular
  • How far players get in challenges
  • Crash reports (to fix bugs fast)
  • General device info (iOS version, device model — never your identity)

All data is anonymous. We cannot identify you.

AdMob (Google) – For Free Play

We show non-intrusive ads so the app stays completely free. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS)
  • Device type, OS version, app usage patterns
  • Approximate location (only if you allow it)

You will see Apple’s App Tracking Transparency prompt the first time you open the app. You can:

  • Allow personalized ads → better-targeted, often higher-quality ads
  • Deny tracking → you’ll still see ads, just less personalized

Full details: Google Privacy Policy

3. Permissions? Only What’s Necessary

We do not ask for access to:

  • Photos / Camera
  • Microphone
  • Location
  • Contacts

No unnecessary permissions = maximum privacy.

4. How to Control Your Data

  • Delete the app → all local data is gone instantly
  • Reset Advertising ID → Settings → Privacy & Security → Advertising → Reset Advertising Identifier
  • Limit Ad Tracking → Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → turn off Personalized Ads

5. Children’s Privacy (COPPA Compliant)

The app is safe and appropriate for teens and adults. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe this has happened, contact us immediately.

6. Changes to This Policy

We’ll update this page if anything changes. The “Last updated” date at the top will always be current.
